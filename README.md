# JioHotstar--Subscription-Funnel-Analysis
Product analysis of JioHotstar's user onboarding and subscription funnel, focusing on user journey mapping, friction points, feature prioritization, and success metrics.

## Project Overview

This project presents a product analysis of JioHotstar's user onboarding and subscription journey, from content discovery to subscription purchase.
The case study identifies potential friction and drop-off points across the user journey and proposes solutions aimed at simplifying the subscription experience, improving conversion, and enhancing user retention.

##  Problem Statement

Users visiting a streaming platform may discover content they want to watch but abandon the journey before completing a subscription.
Potential reasons for drop-off include:
- Unclear subscription benefits
- Multiple steps during signup
- Difficulty comparing available plans
- Payment-related friction
- Limited personalization

  #Key Question: How might we simplify the onboarding and subscription journey to reduce potential user friction, improve subscription conversion, and create a better first-time user experience?

 ## User Personas

Three key user personas were identified:

###  Sports Enthusiast

**Goal:** Watch live cricket and major sporting events.

**Pain Point:** Difficulty identifying which subscription provides access to the desired sports content.

###  Entertainment Viewer

**Goal:** Discover and watch movies and web series.

**Pain Point:** Difficulty evaluating subscription benefits before purchasing.

###  Price-Sensitive User

**Goal:** Access entertainment content at an affordable price.

**Pain Point:** Needs a simple comparison of available plans before making a purchase decision.

---

##  Current User Journey

The existing subscription journey was analyzed across the following stages:

**Open Platform → Browse Content → Select Content → Login / Signup → View Subscription Plans → Select Plan → Complete Payment → Start Watching**

Potential user drop-offs were identified particularly during:

- Account creation
- Plan selection
- Payment

---

##  Identified Pain Points

### 1. Subscription Discovery

Subscription requirements are revealed after content selection, potentially creating unexpected friction in the user journey.

### 2. Plan Selection

Users may find it difficult to clearly compare subscription plans and understand their benefits.

### 3. Signup Friction

Multiple steps before accessing desired content may increase the possibility of user drop-off.

### 4. Payment Failure

Interrupted or failed transactions may cause users to abandon the subscription process.

### 5. Limited Personalization

New users may spend additional time searching for relevant content due to limited initial personalization.

---

##  Proposed Solutions

###  P0 — Simplified Plan Comparison

Provide a clear comparison of subscription plans based on price, content access, supported devices, video quality, and subscription duration.

###  P0 — Payment Recovery Flow

Allow users to retry failed transactions or choose alternative payment methods without restarting the subscription process.

###  P1 — Smart Plan Recommendation

Recommend a suitable subscription plan based on the content selected by the user.

###  P2 — Personalized Onboarding

Allow new users to select their content preferences to improve content discovery and recommendations.

---

##  Feature Prioritization

The proposed solutions were prioritized using the **MoSCoW prioritization framework**.

| Priority | Category | Feature |
|----------|----------|---------|
| P0 | Must Have | Simplified Plan Comparison |
| P0 | Must Have | Payment Recovery Flow |
| P1 | Should Have | Smart Plan Recommendation |
| P2 | Could Have | Personalized Onboarding |
| P3 | Won't Have for Now | Advanced AI-Based Personalization |

---

##  User Stories

### Simplified Plan Comparison

> As a potential subscriber, I want to easily compare subscription plans so that I can select the plan that best suits my needs.

### Payment Recovery Flow

> As a user experiencing a payment failure, I want to quickly retry the transaction or choose an alternative payment method so that I can complete my subscription without restarting the process.

### Smart Plan Recommendation

> As a user, I want to receive a suitable subscription plan recommendation based on the content I select so that I can make a faster and more informed purchase decision.

### Personalized Onboarding

> As a new user, I want to select my content preferences so that I can discover relevant movies, shows, and sports content more easily.

---

##  Proposed User Flow

The proposed optimized journey is:

**Launch App → Explore Library → Choose Content → Get Subscription Recommendation → Sign In → Compare Plans → Complete Transaction → Enjoy Content**

The proposed flow aims to simplify decision-making, improve plan discovery, and reduce potential friction throughout the subscription journey.

---

##  Success Metrics

The following metrics were defined to evaluate the effectiveness of the proposed solutions:

- **Subscription Conversion Rate** — Percentage of users who complete a subscription after entering the subscription funnel.

- **Funnel Drop-Off Rate** — Percentage of users leaving at each stage of the subscription journey.

- **Signup Completion Rate** — Percentage of users who successfully complete the signup process.

- **Payment Success Rate** — Percentage of initiated payment transactions completed successfully.

- **User Retention Rate** — Percentage of users who continue using the platform over a defined period.

---

## Expected Impact

The proposed solutions aim to:

- Reduce friction across the subscription journey
- Simplify subscription plan comparison
- Improve payment recovery
- Enhance content discovery and personalization
- Support improved subscription conversion and retention
